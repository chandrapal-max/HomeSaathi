Built on consent

A guide, not a doctor

CareBridge explains and organizes. It does not diagnose or change treatment, and it always points you back to a qualified doctor.

You asked, we answered.

Is CareBridge a replacement for a doctor?

No. CareBridge is a health literacy and care coordination tool. It explains your documents and helps you prepare, but every medical decision should be made with a qualified doctor.

How does CareBridge handle my data?

Your documents are encrypted and stored privately in an India cloud region. You can export or delete your data at any time, in line with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023.

What documents can I upload?

Lab reports, prescriptions, discharge summaries, and similar text based medical documents, as a PDF or a photo from your phone.

Can my whole family use one account?

Yes. You can create patient profiles for your parents and invite siblings and caregivers with the access you choose.
